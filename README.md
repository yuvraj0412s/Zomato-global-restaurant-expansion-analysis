## 📌 Business Problem

As Asia’s restaurant markets experience rapid growth, major cities are becoming highly saturated. This makes strategic expansion decisions increasingly competitive and high-risk for food delivery platforms and restaurant chains alike.

**The Objective:**
This Business Intelligence project utilizes a comprehensive Zomato global dataset to identify low-competition, high-engagement markets. The goal is to develop data-driven recommendations for Zomato’s executive team regarding where to focus geographic expansion and how to optimize digital adoption (online delivery & table booking) among restaurant partners.

**Expected Outcome:**
Actionable, quantitative insights that minimize market-entry risk and maximize partner onboarding ROI.

---

## 📊 Dataset Overview

The dataset provides a granular view of restaurant performance metrics across multiple geographies.

- **Countries Analyzed:** 15
- **Cities Covered:** 141
- **Total Restaurants:** 9,551
- **Key Features:** User Ratings, Voting Volume, Price Ranges, Cuisine Types, Online Delivery Availability, Table Booking Integration.

## 🔄 Project Workflow

1. **Raw Data Extraction:** Sourced multi-national Zomato restaurant data.
2. **Data Cleaning:** Imputed missing geographical data, standardized currency and pricing schemas.
3. **Feature Engineering:** Developed composite metrics for user engagement and market saturation.
4. **Pivot Tables & Aggregation:** Segmented markets by price range, delivery adoption, and cuisine.
5. **Interactive Dashboarding:** Built dynamic, executive-facing Excel dashboards.
6. **Business Insights:** Extracted trends on pricing vs. ratings and geographic concentration.
7. **Strategic Recommendations:** Formulated consulting-grade market expansion strategies.

---

## 💡 Key Insights

1. **Market Imbalance:** Over 90% of the dataset's restaurants are concentrated in a single market (India), indicating severe saturation in budget segments. Conversely, markets like Qatar and the UAE show lower density but high user engagement.
2. **The "Premium" Rating Effect:** Restaurants in Price Ranges 3 and 4 consistently outperform lower tiers in user ratings, suggesting consumers equate price with quality and are willing to review premium experiences favorably.
3. **The Delivery Disconnect:** A significant percentage of highly-rated, mid-tier restaurants lack online delivery, representing a massive missed revenue opportunity and a clear target for B2B sales teams.

---

## 🚀 Strategic Recommendations

Based on the data analysis, the following actions are recommended for the executive team:

- **Targeted Expansion:** Deprioritize highly saturated budget markets. Immediately allocate resources to expand operations in the UAE, Indonesia, and Qatar, capitalizing on high engagement and low competition.
- **B2B Sales Optimization:** Deploy targeted sales initiatives aimed at Price Range 2 and 3 restaurants lacking online delivery. Use case studies to demonstrate ROI on digital integration.
- **Enhance Premium Features:** Given the high performance of expensive restaurants, invest in premium platform features like VIP Table Booking tiers to capture high-value diners.
