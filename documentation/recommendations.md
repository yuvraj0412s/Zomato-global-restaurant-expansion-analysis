# Strategic Recommendations

## 1. Targeted Geographic Expansion
**Focus on Emerging Markets:** Prioritize expansion in the UAE, Indonesia, and Qatar. These regions demonstrate lower competitive saturation combined with high average consumer ratings and votes, offering a lucrative environment for market entry and rapid user acquisition.

## 2. B2B Merchant Onboarding Strategy
**Capitalize on the Delivery Gap:** Deploy targeted sales teams to onboard mid-tier restaurants (Price Range 2 and 3) that currently lack online delivery capabilities. Highlighting case studies of increased revenue through digital integration will drive adoption.

## 3. Premium Segment Optimization
**Enhance Table Booking Features:** Since higher-priced restaurants (Price Range 3 and 4) correlate with higher ratings and customer loyalty, enhance the "Table Booking" feature suite. Introduce VIP reservation tiers or loyalty rewards to capture and retain high-value diners.

## 4. Market-Specific Adaptation
**Hyper-Local Customization:** While scaling globally, tailor the platform interface and promotions to highlight region-specific dominant cuisines, balancing international fast food demand with local culinary preferences.
