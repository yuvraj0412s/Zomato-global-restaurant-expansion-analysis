# Business Insights

## 1. Market Saturation & Concentration
- **Highly Saturated Markets**: India dominates the dataset with over 90% of listed restaurants, heavily concentrated in Price Range 1, indicating intense competition in the budget dining segment. The US also shows high saturation.
- **Emerging Opportunities**: Markets like Indonesia, Philippines, Qatar, and UAE show significantly lower restaurant counts but maintain healthy customer engagement (measured by average ratings and votes). These represent prime targets for Zomato's strategic expansion.

## 2. Customer Preferences & Pricing
- **Rating Trends**: Higher-priced restaurants (Price Range 3 & 4) consistently achieve better ratings globally, suggesting that customers equate higher cost with premium dining experiences.
- **Cuisine Demand**: Local cuisines dominate their respective regions, but broadly appealing categories like "North Indian", "Chinese", and "Fast Food" maintain strong, consistent global traction.

## 3. Delivery & Digital Adoption
- **Online Delivery Gap**: While table booking is a premium feature, online delivery is increasingly becoming a baseline expectation.
- **Strategic Disconnect**: Despite high demand for convenience, a significant portion of mid-tier restaurants still lack online delivery integration, presenting a B2B onboarding opportunity for Zomato's sales teams.
